ABOUT ME
Hello! My name is Lera Dopirchuk. I am a professional photographer from Ukraine.
I was born in Chernihiv, graduated from school, danced for a long time in the "Curts" group.
Then I received higher education with a degree in choreographer-balletmaster at the Kyiv National University of Culture and Arts
From my mother, who was engaged in rhythmic gymnastics, I inherited a penchant for dancing. My uncle was engaged in photography,
but at an amateur level. Many of the shootings that I conducted in my home town and other cities convey the aesthetics and spirit of the 90s,
and although my conscious age fell on the 2000s. I explain this by the fact that much remains in my environment that connects me with the past and
the older generation.
I get inspired by inspiring other people. I am very glad that my work has become my work.
Thanks to this, I can spread creativity and collaborate with many creative individuals and organizations.
I prefer making streetstyle pictures and editing them in my unique style. Also I like love stories.
I am trying to show my inner world and raw emotions through my work.
If you like my style, you can contact me and we will agree on cooperation.
